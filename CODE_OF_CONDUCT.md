# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, or religion.

This code of conduct outlines our expectations for your behavior as a contributor to our project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of others.
- Do not harass or abuse others.
- Do not use offensive language.
- Do not make personal attacks.
- Do not troll or spam.
- Do not post any personal information about others.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Any behavior that violates our code of conduct will not be tolerated.
- We reserve the right to remove any content that we deem to be inappropriate.
- We also reserve the right to ban anyone who violates our code of conduct.

## Reporting Violations

If you witness any violations of our code of conduct, please report them to us immediately. You can do so by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
